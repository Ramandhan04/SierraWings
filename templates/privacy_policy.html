Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data protection is our priority

Last Updated: June 13, 2025

1. Introduction

SierraWings, developed by Sahid R.M Dumbuya and Maada Lumeh, is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our emergency medical drone delivery platform.

Our Privacy Commitment
  • We never sell your personal or medical information
  • Your data is encrypted using industry-leading security standards
  • We comply with Sierra Leone and international privacy laws
  • You have full control over your personal information
  • Medical data is handled with the highest level of confidentiality

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information
  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password
  • Identity Verification: Government ID for clinic registration
  • Contact Details: Physical address, emergency contact information
  • Profile Information: User role (patient, clinic, admin), preferences
2.2 Medical Information
Protected Health Information (PHI)
  • Medical supply requests and prescription information
  • Health conditions relevant to delivery requirements
  • Medication history and allergy information
  • Emergency medical contact details
  • Clinic specialties and medical license information
2.3 Technical Information
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Platform interactions, feature usage, session duration
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates for delivery addresses
  • Communication Records: Support messages, feedback submissions
2.4 Financial Information
  • Payment Methods: Mobile money numbers, card tokens (encrypted)
  • Transaction History: Payment records, delivery fees
  • Billing Information: Invoice details, payment status

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Services
  • Processing and coordinating emergency medical deliveries
  • Connecting patients with appropriate medical clinics
  • Real-time tracking and monitoring of drone deliveries
  • Secure payment processing and billing
  • Emergency response coordination and communication
3.2 Platform Improvement
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Developing new features based on user feedback
  • Improving delivery efficiency and route optimization
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing fraud
3.3 Communication
  • Sending delivery notifications and status updates
  • Providing customer support and technical assistance
  • Sharing important safety and health information
  • Delivering service announcements and updates

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

When We Share Information

We only share your information in these specific circumstances:

  • Medical Clinics: Relevant medical information for treatment
  • Payment Processors: Encrypted payment data for transactions
  • Emergency Services: Critical information during medical emergencies
  • Legal Requirements: When required by Sierra Leone law
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners under strict data agreements
4.1 Third-Party Services

We work with carefully selected third-party services:

  • Mobile Money Providers: Afri Money, Orange Money, Q-Money for payments
  • Mapping Services: For delivery route optimization and tracking
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Secure data storage and platform hosting
  • Email Services: For important notifications and communications

5. Data Security Measures

Advanced Security Protocols
Encryption & Protection
  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • AES-256 encryption for stored data
  • End-to-end encryption for medical communications
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
Access Controls
  • Multi-factor authentication for sensitive accounts
  • Role-based access control systems
  • Regular access reviews and permission updates
  • Secure user session management

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Control
  • Data Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Data Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Communication Preferences: Control how we contact you
6.2 Medical Privacy Rights
  • View your medical delivery history
  • Control who has access to your medical information
  • Request restrictions on medical data sharing
  • Receive notifications about medical data access

7. Data Retention

Data Retention Periods
  • Account Information: Retained while account is active + 3 years
  • Medical Records: 7 years (as required by medical regulations)
  • Transaction Data: 5 years (financial compliance requirements)
  • Technical Logs: 2 years for security and troubleshooting
  • Communication Records: 3 years for support and quality assurance

8. Children's Privacy

SierraWings is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Medical deliveries for minors must be requested by parents or legal guardians. If we become aware of children's data collection, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored within Sierra Leone. When international transfers are necessary for technical operations, we ensure:

  • Adequate protection levels in destination countries
  • Secure transfer mechanisms and encryption
  • Contractual obligations for data protection
  • Compliance with international privacy frameworks

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session securely
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze platform usage for improvements
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

11. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notifications to registered users
  • Platform announcements and alerts
  • Updated version posted on our website
  • 30-day notice period for major changes

12. Contact Us

Privacy Questions & Requests

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights:

  • Privacy Officer: privacy@sierrawings.com
  • Data Protection: dataprotection@sierrawings.com
  • Security Issues: security@sierrawings.com
  • General Support: support@sierrawings.com
  • Postal Address: SierraWings Privacy Team, Freetown, Sierra Leone
  • Response Time: 5-7 business days for privacy requests
Legal Compliance & Regulations

SierraWings privacy practices comply with:

  • Sierra Leone Data Protection Act
  • Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) principles
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) standards
  • African Union Convention on Cyber Security and Personal Data Protection
  • Sierra Leone Medical and Dental Council regulations
